Excess Drinking Behavior Among Young Adults - Research Project

This research project delves into the complex world of young adults and their relationship with alcohol. It examines the significance of alcohol consumption in their daily lives, shedding light on the behaviors and experiences of "ordinary" young adult drinkers. The study covers a wide range of factors, from alcohol consumption frequency and quantity to the motivations behind their drinking habits. The aim is to unravel the intricate interplay between alcohol, peer influence, emotional well-being, and its effects on young individuals. Key findings include insights into binge drinking prevalence, the role of peer pressure, and the impact of alcohol on social interactions and emotional states.

NumPy || Pandas || Python || Matplotlib || Excel || Survey Questionnaire
